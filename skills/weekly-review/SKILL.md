# Weekly Review

1. Read only measured values; keep missing metrics explicit.
2. Report the two Phase 1 KPIs first: monthly new followers and qualified calendar visits.
3. Summarize content mix as guidance, not a posting quota.
4. List effective and ineffective drafts, rejection reasons, unresolved rights reviews, input gaps and next-week candidates.
5. Describe A/B results cautiously and only after the configured minimum sample/time.
6. Put every suggested KPI, frequency, tone, rights, approval or mix change under “Human decision required.”

Do not mutate configuration or create low-quality posts to fill a ratio.
