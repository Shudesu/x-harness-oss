# Content Guidelines

Every draft must identify its event, content category, destination and rights evidence. Use the deterministic templates as a starting point, then keep the artist voice factual, warm and concise.

- Never imply that an upcoming show already happened.
- Do not expose private attendee, staff, travel or backstage information.
- Do not publish media without valid ownership/license evidence and an unexpired usage window.
- Avoid duplicate copy, engagement bait, unsolicited replies/DMs and automated engagement.
- Build tracking parameters from the canonical HTTPS destination; do not paste pre-tracked URLs.
- Keep copy within the server-validated X weighted-text limit; Japanese/CJK characters do not count the same as ASCII.
- Drafts scoring below 80, stale drafts, ambiguous rights or rejected privacy checks cannot be approved.

Category mix and account voice are configured in `config/content-mix.yaml`, `config/strategy.yaml` and `config/account-profile.yaml`. Open interpretation questions belong in `docs/Decision-Grill.md`.
